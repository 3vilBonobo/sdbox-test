SDBOX is an additional software (Add-on) for CAD platforms and provides tools that help the processing and management of the design and drafting workflows of your projects.

The past 10 years our main concern, as a software development company, has been to provide the best possible support to our customers. Your projects are our projects!

Our motives for developing sdbox are:

  • The continuous evolution of the successful and popular greek version to reach international users.
  • The automation of frequently repeated design processes.
  • The standardization of designs using templates process.
  • Sharing tools for symbols, texts, numbers, dimensions, etc.
  • An easy way to create and manage your libraries.

CONTENTS

Math calculations

Automatic presentations of calculations of surfaces, lengths and on objects, automatic dimensioning, deviation calculations, slopes, etc.

Plots and Buildings

Topographic-Survey Plans, Floor Plans-Elevation Plans-Section Plans, Building Plans, Fire Protection, Land Registry Plans, Site Support Plans, Photo Documentation etc

Design automations

Counting, measurements, legends, tables, mass object changes, etc.

Design management

Mass conversions / corrections / additions / replacements / export-import of texts and symbols, management-adjustment of images, line conversions, layer management, etc.

Files and Folders

File import / export, file editor, automatic management of folders, etc.

Transfer your projects to Google Earth

Select objects (lines, plines, splines, arcs, circles, ellipses, points, blocks, texts, images etc.) from the AutoCAD app and export them to GE preserving the design layers.

Print

Automatic print to pdf for common paper sizes and export dxf to a new design, preset ctb's for each scale, etc.
